\section{Lepton Veto}
\subsection{Motivation}
\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Motivation}
\begin{itemize}
  \item   Choosing fine granularity of search bins in \HT, \MHT, \NJets and BTags challenges methods relaying on control samples (CS)
  \item   The `classical` lost-lepton method relayed on a single muon control sample selected and predicting for each search bin
  \item   In 8\tev the dominant uncertainty for some of the most sensitive bins was statistics of $\mu$ CS
  \item   Same restriction expected for 13\tev
  \item   Idea: Use also single electron CS for prediction
  \item   Reduction of uncertainty ~$\sqrt{2}$
\end{itemize} 
\end{frame}
